# **ChestSignLocker**

## Protect your chests with a simple sign
No commands, no complex setup — just place a wall sign on any chest, and it's locked to you instantly.

---

## **How to use**
Place any wall sign on a chest. It will automatically display **[Protected]** with your name, and no other player will be able to open it, break it, or steal from it using hoppers.

To remove the protection, simply break the sign or the chest yourself.

---

## **Features**
- Single and double chest support
- Protection extends automatically when you expand a single chest into a double chest
- Hopper and Hopper Minecart theft prevention
- Server broadcast when a player attempts to steal from a protected chest
- Protection data saved to disk — survives server restarts
- No commands required
- Lightweight — no dependencies

---

## **Compatibility**
- Minecraft 1.21+
- Paper / Spigot
